# Business Context

**Industry:** Media / Social Good / Journalism

**Target Users:** News organizations, social media platforms, fact-checking services, journalists, and the general public.

**Impact:** By automating detection of misinformation, NeuralTruth helps platforms reduce the spread of harmful content, builds public trust in information systems, and assists journalists in rapid fact-checking workflows.

**Revenue Model:** API access subscriptions for B2B clients (news portals, platforms) and freemium individual accounts. AdSense-ready frontend for public traffic.
