# EDA Notes

## Key Findings
- Dataset: 44,919 articles (23,502 Fake + 21,417 Real)
- Average article length: ~850 words
- Most common fake news topics: Politics, World, Government conspiracies
- Most common real news topics: Economy, Science, Health
