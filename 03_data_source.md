# Data Sources

1. **Kaggle Fake and Real News Dataset** — 44,919 articles labeled Fake/Real.
   - Source: https://www.kaggle.com/clmentbisaillon/fake-and-real-news-dataset

2. **LIAR Dataset** — 12,836 statements labeled with fine-grained truthfulness scores.
   - Source: https://huggingface.co/datasets/liar

3. **WELFake Dataset** — 72,134 news articles for text classification.
   - Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/saurabhshahane/fake-news-classification

Place raw CSV files in the `04_data_collection/` folder.
